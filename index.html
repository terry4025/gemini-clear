i How it works

Local cleanup with a reversible mask

GeminiClear uses a bundled OpenCV.js build in your browser. Manual strokes and auto-detected suggestions are rendered into a full-resolution mask, then processed with TELEA inpainting for the final PNG export.

Full-resolution export

The preview is downscaled for interactive painting, but the downloaded PNG always keeps the original image dimensions.

Manual + auto mask

Paint corrections by hand, or let the bottom-right detector suggest likely watermark fragments without touching your manual edits.

Current limits

Detection is optimized for the typical small Gemini mark in the bottom-right corner. Complex overlays may still need manual masking.
